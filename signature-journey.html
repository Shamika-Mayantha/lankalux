Signature Sri Lanka Journey

A comprehensive 10-day journey showcasing the best of Sri Lanka's culture, nature, wildlife, and coastal beauty.

01
Arrival in Colombo

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you'll be greeted by your private chauffeur and transferred to your hotel in Colombo.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Private transfer to Colombo
  • Evening at leisure to recover from your journey
  • Optional: Brief city tour of Colombo
02
Colombo to Sigiriya

After breakfast, journey to the Cultural Triangle. Visit the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage site rising 200 meters above the surrounding plains.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive through rural Sri Lanka
  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore ancient frescoes and water gardens
  • Sunset views from the summit
03
Cultural Triangle Exploration

Discover the ancient cities of Polonnaruwa and Anuradhapura. Explore well-preserved ruins, massive stupas, and intricate stone carvings that tell the story of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.

Highlights:

  • Polonnaruwa ancient city tour
  • Anuradhapura sacred city visit
  • Buddhist temples and stupas
  • Ancient irrigation systems
04
Sigiriya to Kandy

Travel to the hill capital of Kandy, home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic. En route, visit a spice garden to learn about Sri Lankan spices and traditional medicine.

Highlights:

  • Spice garden visit
  • Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Kandy Lake and city tour
  • Traditional Kandyan dance performance
05
Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

Journey into the tea country, ascending through misty hills and emerald-green tea plantations. Visit a working tea factory and enjoy a cup of freshly brewed Ceylon tea.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive through tea plantations
  • Tea factory tour and tasting
  • Nuwara Eliya hill station
  • Gregory Lake and colonial architecture
06
Nuwara Eliya to Yala

Descend from the hills to the wildlife-rich plains of Yala National Park. Check into your safari lodge and prepare for an early morning game drive.

Highlights:

  • Scenic journey through diverse landscapes
  • Arrival at Yala National Park
  • Safari lodge accommodation
  • Evening at leisure
07
Yala Wildlife Safari

Early morning and evening game drives in Yala National Park, one of the best places in the world to spot leopards. The park is also home to elephants, sloth bears, and diverse birdlife.

Highlights:

  • Early morning game drive (5:30 AM)
  • Leopard spotting opportunities
  • Elephant herds and bird watching
  • Evening game drive
08
Yala to Galle / Mirissa

Travel to the southern coast, stopping at the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Continue to the beach town of Mirissa for relaxation and coastal beauty.

Highlights:

  • Galle Fort exploration
  • Dutch colonial architecture
  • Coastal drive to Mirissa
  • Beach relaxation
09
Coastal Relaxation

A day to unwind on the beautiful beaches of Mirissa. Optional activities include whale watching (seasonal), surfing, or simply enjoying the golden sands and turquoise waters.

Highlights:

  • Beach relaxation
  • Optional whale watching tour
  • Water sports activities
  • Fresh seafood dining
10
Departure

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, taking with you unforgettable memories of your Signature Sri Lanka Journey.

Highlights:

  • Private transfer to airport
  • Farewell from your chauffeur
  • Departure assistance
