Coastal Retreat Journey

A relaxing 6-day coastal escape exploring Sri Lanka's stunning beaches, from the historic Galle Fort to pristine bays perfect for swimming, surfing, and sunset watching.

01
Arrival & Transfer to Galle

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your private chauffeur. Transfer to Galle, a charming coastal town with a rich colonial history. Check into your hotel in or near the historic Galle Fort.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Scenic coastal drive
  • Galle Fort arrival
  • Evening at leisure
02
Galle Fort Exploration

Spend the day exploring Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Walk along the ramparts with ocean views, visit Dutch colonial buildings, browse boutique shops, and enjoy cafes and restaurants. The fort is a perfect blend of history and modern charm.

Highlights:

  • Galle Fort walking tour
  • Dutch colonial architecture
  • Rampart walk with ocean views
  • Boutique shopping and cafes
  • Lighthouse visit
03
Galle to Mirissa

Travel to Mirissa, a beautiful beach town known for its golden sands and excellent whale watching. Check into your beachfront accommodation and spend the afternoon relaxing on the beach or by the pool.

Highlights:

  • Short drive to Mirissa
  • Beachfront accommodation
  • Beach relaxation
  • Sunset watching
04
Mirissa - Whale Watching & Beach

Early morning whale watching tour (seasonal, November-April) to spot blue whales and dolphins. After returning, enjoy the rest of the day at leisure - relax on the beach, try surfing, or explore the local area. Enjoy fresh seafood at a beachside restaurant.

Highlights:

  • Whale watching tour (seasonal)
  • Dolphin spotting
  • Beach activities
  • Surfing (optional)
  • Fresh seafood dining
05
Mirissa to Arugam Bay

Journey to Arugam Bay on the east coast, one of Asia's best surfing destinations. The drive takes you through diverse landscapes. Arugam Bay offers pristine beaches, excellent surf breaks, and a laid-back atmosphere.

Highlights:

  • Scenic coastal drive
  • Arrival in Arugam Bay
  • Beachfront check-in
  • Evening beach walk
06
Arugam Bay - Beach & Departure

Enjoy a final morning on the beautiful beaches of Arugam Bay. Optional activities include surfing lessons, lagoon boat rides, or simply relaxing. After lunch, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, refreshed and rejuvenated.

Highlights:

  • Beach relaxation
  • Surfing (optional)
  • Lagoon activities
  • Transfer to airport
  • Departure
