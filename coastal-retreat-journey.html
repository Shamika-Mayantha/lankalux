Coastal Retreat Journey

A relaxing 8-day coastal escape exploring Sri Lanka's stunning beaches, from the historic Galle Fort to pristine bays perfect for swimming, surfing, and sunset watching.

01
Arrival & Transfer to Arugam Bay

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your private chauffeur. Begin the cross-island journey to Arugam Bay, passing changing landscapes. Upon arrival, check in and enjoy a relaxed evening to recover from your travel day.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Cross-island scenic drive
  • Hotel check-in in Arugam Bay
  • Evening at leisure
02
Arugam Bay – Surf & Beach Day

A full day to enjoy Arugam Bay at your own pace. Spend time on the beach, try surfing with optional lessons, and enjoy the relaxed coastal atmosphere.

Highlights:

  • Surfing (optional)
  • Beach relaxation
  • Coastal cafés
  • Sunset by the ocean
03
Arugam Bay – Leisure Day

Another relaxed day on the east coast. Enjoy slow beach time, swimming, or take an optional lagoon or village visit nearby. The day remains flexible and unhurried.

Highlights:

  • Free beach time
  • Swimming and sunbathing
  • Optional lagoon or village visit
  • Relaxed evening
04
Arugam Bay to Mirissa

Depart Arugam Bay early and travel to the south coast. Arrive in Mirissa by afternoon and settle into your beachfront stay. Enjoy a calm evening by the sea.

Highlights:

  • Scenic cross-country drive
  • Arrival on the south coast
  • Beachfront hotel check-in
  • Evening at leisure
05
Mirissa – Beach & Optional Whale Watching

A relaxed day in Mirissa. Optional whale watching is available seasonally (November to April). Otherwise, enjoy beach time, swimming, or local cafés.

Highlights:

  • Optional whale watching (seasonal)
  • Beach relaxation
  • Swimming and sunbathing
  • Fresh seafood dining
06
Mirissa to Galle

After breakfast, enjoy a short coastal drive to Galle. Check in and explore the historic fort area at a relaxed pace.

Highlights:

  • Short coastal transfer
  • Hotel check-in in Galle
  • Galle Fort exploration
  • Sunset rampart walk
07
Galle Fort – Leisure Day

A full relaxed day in Galle Fort. Walk the ramparts, browse boutiques, enjoy cafés, and soak in the coastal atmosphere.

Highlights:

  • Galle Fort at leisure
  • Ocean-view ramparts
  • Boutique shopping
  • Easy final evening
08
Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your departure flight.

Highlights:

  • Relaxed morning
  • Private airport transfer
  • Departure assistance
