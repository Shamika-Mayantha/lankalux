Hill Country & Scenic Rail Journey

A 8-day journey through Sri Lanka's misty highlands, featuring the world's most scenic train ride, tea plantations, and the cool climate of hill country towns.

01
Arrival & Transfer to Kandy

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your private chauffeur. Travel at a relaxed pace to Kandy and check into your hotel overlooking the lake. The remainder of the day is free to rest and unwind.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Scenic drive to Kandy
  • Hotel check-in
  • Evening at leisure
02
Kandy Culture & Relaxation

Explore Kandy at an easy pace. Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, enjoy a walk around Kandy Lake, and explore the Royal Botanical Gardens. Return to your hotel in the afternoon for rest or relaxation.

Highlights:

  • Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Kandy Lake walk
  • Royal Botanical Gardens
  • Free afternoon
03
Scenic Train Journey to Nuwara Eliya

Board the scenic hill-country train from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya, passing tea estates, misty mountains, and waterfalls. On arrival, your chauffeur will meet you and transfer you to your hotel.

Highlights:

  • Scenic train journey
  • Tea plantation views
  • Chauffeur pick-up
  • Evening at leisure
04
Nuwara Eliya

Enjoy a relaxed day in Nuwara Eliya. Visit a tea factory, stroll around Gregory Lake, and explore the town‚Äôs colonial charm. The afternoon is free to rest or enjoy the cool mountain climate.

Highlights:

  • Tea factory visit
  • Gregory Lake walk
  • Colonial town exploration
  • Free afternoon
05
Nuwara Eliya to Ella

Travel through scenic hill-country landscapes to Ella. Visit the famous Nine Arch Bridge and settle into your hotel surrounded by mountain views.

Highlights:

  • Scenic hill-country drive
  • Nine Arch Bridge
  • Hotel check-in
  • Relaxed evening
06
Ella at Leisure

A full day to enjoy Ella at your own pace. Optional visits include Little Adam‚Äôs Peak or Ravana Falls. Spend the remainder of the day relaxing or exploring the town.

Highlights:

  • Optional Little Adam‚Äôs Peak
  • Ravana Falls
  • Free time
  • Hill-country views
07
Ella to West Coast / Near Airport

After breakfast, depart Ella and travel towards the west coast. Enjoy a comfortable drive through changing landscapes and check into your hotel near the coast. Evening at leisure.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive from hill country
  • Hotel check-in near airport
  • Time to rest
  • Relaxed final evening
08
Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a short and smooth transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

Highlights:

  • Breakfast
  • Short airport transfer
  • Departure assistance
