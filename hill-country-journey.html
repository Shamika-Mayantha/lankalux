Hill Country & Scenic Rail Journey

A 7-day journey through Sri Lanka's misty highlands, featuring the world's most scenic train ride, tea plantations, and the cool climate of hill country towns.

01
Arrival & Transfer to Kandy

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your private chauffeur. Transfer to Kandy, the cultural capital nestled in the hills. Check into your hotel overlooking Kandy Lake.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Scenic drive to Kandy
  • Hotel check-in
  • Evening at leisure
02
Kandy Cultural Exploration

Explore Kandy's cultural treasures. Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of Buddhism's most sacred sites. Stroll around Kandy Lake and visit the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya, home to over 4,000 species of plants.

Highlights:

  • Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Kandy Lake walk
  • Royal Botanical Gardens
  • Traditional Kandyan dance performance
03
Scenic Train Journey to Nuwara Eliya

Experience one of the world's most beautiful train journeys from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya. Wind through emerald-green tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls. Your chauffeur will meet you at the station.

Highlights:

  • Scenic train journey (3-4 hours)
  • Tea plantation views
  • Mountain landscapes
  • Arrival in Nuwara Eliya
04
Nuwara Eliya - Little England

Explore Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England" for its colonial architecture and cool climate. Visit a working tea factory, learn about the tea-making process, and enjoy a cup of freshly brewed Ceylon tea. Walk through Gregory Lake and explore the town's colonial heritage.

Highlights:

  • Tea factory tour and tasting
  • Gregory Lake visit
  • Colonial architecture tour
  • Tea plantation walk
05
Nuwara Eliya to Ella

Drive to Ella, a charming hill town with stunning views. Visit the famous Nine Arch Bridge, an engineering marvel built during the British colonial era. Hike to Little Adam's Peak for panoramic views of the tea country.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive through tea country
  • Nine Arch Bridge visit
  • Little Adam's Peak hike
  • Ella town exploration
06
Ella - Nature & Views

Full day in Ella. Optional hike to Ella Rock for breathtaking sunrise views, or visit Ravana Falls and the Ravana Cave. Enjoy the cool mountain air and stunning landscapes. Experience local cuisine at a hill country restaurant.

Highlights:

  • Ella Rock hike (optional)
  • Ravana Falls visit
  • Mountain viewpoints
  • Local cuisine experience
07
Ella to Airport & Departure

After breakfast, begin your journey back to the airport. Stop at a spice garden en route to learn about Sri Lankan spices. Arrive at the airport with plenty of time for your departure flight, carrying memories of misty mountains and scenic train rides.

Highlights:

  • Spice garden visit
  • Scenic drive to airport
  • Transfer assistance
  • Departure
