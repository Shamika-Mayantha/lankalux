Nature & Wildlife Discovery Journey

An immersive 9-day adventure focusing on Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity, from leopards and elephants to diverse birdlife and pristine natural habitats.

01
Arrival in Sri Lanka & Overnight Near Airport

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by your private chauffeur. After assistance with arrival formalities, transfer to a comfortable hotel near the airport or along the west coast to rest and recover from your international journey.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Private transfer to hotel
  • Time to relax and refresh
  • Overnight near airport for a smooth start
02
Transfer to Yala National Park

After breakfast, depart towards Yala National Park, traveling through changing landscapes from coastal plains to dry-zone wilderness. Upon arrival, check into your safari lodge and enjoy time at leisure before your wildlife adventure begins.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive to Yala National Park
  • Safari lodge check-in
  • Afternoon at leisure
  • Evening briefing on safari activities
03
Yala National Park | Full-Day Safari

Spend the day exploring Yala National Park, renowned for having one of the highest leopard densities in the world. Enjoy early morning and afternoon game drives led by experienced trackers, with opportunities to spot elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and diverse birdlife.

Highlights:

  • Early morning game drive
  • Leopard and elephant sightings
  • Birdwatching and photography
  • Afternoon safari through varied habitats
04
Yala to Udawalawe National Park

Travel onward to Udawalawe National Park, famous for its large elephant population and open grasslands that provide excellent wildlife viewing opportunities. En route, visit the Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home.

Highlights:

  • Visit to Elephant Transit Home
  • Scenic countryside drive
  • Evening safari in Udawalawe
  • Close encounters with elephant herds
05
Udawalawe to Sinharaja Rainforest

Journey towards the lush rainforests of Sinharaja, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka‚Äôs richest biodiversity zones. Upon arrival, check into your rainforest lodge and enjoy a gentle evening nature walk.

Highlights:

  • Scenic transfer to Sinharaja
  • Rainforest lodge accommodation
  • Evening nature walk
  • Introduction to endemic wildlife
06
Sinharaja Rainforest Exploration

Explore Sinharaja with an expert naturalist guide. Trek through dense rainforest trails, search for endemic bird species, observe rare flora, and visit hidden waterfalls. Activities are paced according to forest conditions.

Highlights:

  • Early morning birdwatching
  • Guided rainforest trek
  • Endemic species spotting
  • Waterfall visit
  • Evening nature presentation
07
Sinharaja to Wilpattu National Park

Depart Sinharaja and travel north to Wilpattu National Park, Sri Lanka‚Äôs largest and oldest national park. Known for its natural villus (lakes), Wilpattu offers a quieter and more secluded safari experience. Check in and enjoy an evening game drive.

Highlights:

  • Scenic long-distance journey
  • Wilpattu safari lodge check-in
  • Evening game drive
  • Wildlife photography opportunities
08
Wilpattu Safari & Transfer to West Coast

Enjoy an early morning safari in Wilpattu, searching for leopards, sloth bears, and other wildlife around the park‚Äôs villus. After a relaxed breakfast, begin your journey back towards the west coast for a comfortable overnight stay near the airport.

Highlights:

  • Early morning game drive
  • Final wildlife sightings in Wilpattu
  • Scenic transfer to the west coast
  • Overnight stay near the airport for a relaxed departure the next day
09
Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a smooth transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your departure flight. Your chauffeur will assist with drop-off arrangements, concluding your Nature & Wildlife Discovery Journey with ease.

Highlights:

  • Relaxed morning near the airport
  • Private transfer to the airport
  • Departure assistance
  • Farewell from your chauffeur
