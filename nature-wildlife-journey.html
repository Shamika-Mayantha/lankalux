Nature & Wildlife Discovery Journey

An immersive 8-day adventure focusing on Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity, from leopards and elephants to diverse birdlife and pristine natural habitats.

01
Arrival & Transfer to Yala

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your private chauffeur. Begin your wildlife journey with a scenic drive to Yala National Park, one of the best places in the world to spot leopards.

Highlights:

  • Airport meet & greet
  • Private transfer to Yala
  • Check into safari lodge
  • Evening briefing on safari activities
02
Yala National Park - Full Day Safari

Early morning game drive (5:30 AM) when wildlife is most active. Return for breakfast, then afternoon game drive. Yala is home to the highest density of leopards in the world, plus elephants, sloth bears, and over 200 bird species.

Highlights:

  • Early morning game drive (5:30 AM - 9:30 AM)
  • Leopard spotting opportunities
  • Elephant herds and bird watching
  • Afternoon game drive (3:00 PM - 6:30 PM)
03
Yala to Udawalawe

Travel to Udawalawe National Park, famous for its large elephant population. En route, visit the Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home, where orphaned elephants are cared for before being released back into the wild.

Highlights:

  • Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home visit
  • Elephant feeding observation
  • Transfer to Udawalawe
  • Evening game drive in Udawalawe
04
Udawalawe National Park

Full day exploring Udawalawe, where you're almost guaranteed to see elephants. The park's open grasslands make wildlife viewing excellent. Spot water buffalo, spotted deer, and numerous bird species including eagles and kingfishers.

Highlights:

  • Morning game drive
  • Elephant herds in natural habitat
  • Bird watching (200+ species)
  • Evening game drive
05
Udawalawe to Sinharaja

Journey to Sinharaja Forest Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the last remaining primary rainforests in Sri Lanka. This biodiversity hotspot is home to many endemic species.

Highlights:

  • Scenic drive to Sinharaja
  • Rainforest lodge check-in
  • Evening nature walk
  • Bird watching preparation
06
Sinharaja Rainforest Exploration

Full day exploring Sinharaja with an expert naturalist guide. This pristine rainforest is home to 95% of Sri Lanka's endemic birds, plus rare mammals, reptiles, and countless plant species. Trek through ancient forest trails.

Highlights:

  • Early morning bird watching
  • Guided rainforest trek
  • Endemic species spotting
  • Waterfall visit
  • Evening nature presentation
07
Sinharaja to Wilpattu

Travel to Wilpattu National Park, the largest and oldest national park in Sri Lanka. Known for its natural "villus" (lakes), Wilpattu offers a different safari experience with excellent leopard and sloth bear viewing opportunities.

Highlights:

  • Scenic journey to Wilpattu
  • Safari camp check-in
  • Evening game drive
  • Wildlife photography opportunities
08
Wilpattu Safari & Departure

Early morning game drive in Wilpattu, searching for leopards, sloth bears, and other wildlife. After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, enriched by unforgettable wildlife encounters.

Highlights:

  • Early morning game drive
  • Final wildlife sightings
  • Transfer to airport
  • Departure assistance
